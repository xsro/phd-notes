\section{问题描述}

考虑 \(N\) 个智能体和一个目标。设智能体 \(i\) 的位置、速度分别为
\[
    p_i(t)\in\mathbb R^m,\qquad v_i(t)\in\mathbb R^m,
\]
目标的位置、速度分别为
\[
    p_0(t)\in\mathbb R^m,\qquad v_0(t)\in\mathbb R^m.
\]

考虑智能体 \(i\) 的二阶动力学为
\begin{align}
    \dot p_i &= v_i, \label{eq:agent-p}\\
    \dot v_i &= B_i(t)u_i+d_i, \label{eq:agent-v}
\end{align}
其中
\begin{itemize}[nosep]
    \item \(u_i\in\mathbb R^m\) 是控制输入，
    \item \(d_i\in\mathbb R^m\) 是有界扰动，
    \item \(B_i(t)\in\mathbb R^{m\times m}\) 是未知输入增益矩阵。
\end{itemize}

为了使用逐分量周期滑模控制，假设 \(B_i(t)\) 具有对角结构：
\[
    B_i(t)=\operatorname{diag}\{b_{i1}(t),\dots,b_{im}(t)\}.
\]
其中每个 \(b_{i\ell}(t)\) 的符号未知，并允许在正负之间切换，但不允许穿越零。具体假设为：存在已知常数 \(\underline b_{i\ell}>0\) 使得
\(
    0<\underline b_{i\ell}\le |b_{i\ell}(t)|,\qquad \ell=1,\dots,m.
\)
扰动满足
\(
    \|d_i(t)\|_\infty\le \bar d_i.
\)

目标系统为
\begin{align}
    \dot p_0 &= v_0, \\
    \dot v_0 &= u_0,
\end{align}
其中目标加速度有界：
\(
    \|u_0(t)\|_\infty\le \bar u_0.
\)

定义智能体 \(i\) 关于目标的相对位置和相对速度为
\(p_{i0}=p_i-p_0,\)
\(v_{i0}=v_i-v_0\).
于是
\begin{align}
    \dot p_{i0} &= v_{i0}, \\
    \dot v_{i0} &= B_i(t)u_i+d_i-u_0.
\end{align}

定义\( w_i = d_i-u_0\).
由扰动和目标加速度有界性，有
\(
    \|w_i(t)\|_\infty \le \bar d_i+\bar u_0.
\)
记 \(\bar w_i=\bar d_i+\bar u_0\)，因此对每个分量有
\[
    |w_{i\ell}(t)|\le \bar w_i.
\]


The fencing formation control objective considered in this paper is to achieve the following properties:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item[\textbf{P1}] (Convex-hull Fencing) The target is asymptotically fenced by the convex hull spanned by the satellites, i.e., 
    $\lim_{t \to \infty} \mathrm{dist} \bigl( p_0(t), \mathrm{co} (p(t)) \bigr) = 0$.
    
    \item[\textbf{P2}] (Collision Avoidance) The inter-satellite distances remain above the prescribed safety distance under the closed-loop dynamics, i.e., if $\| p_i(0)-p_j(0) \| > d$, then $\| p_i(t)-p_j(t) \| > d$ for all $t \ge 0$ and all $i, j \in \mathcal N$ with $i \ne j$.

    \item[\textbf{P3}] (Velocity Consensus) The velocity of each satellite asymptotically converges to the target velocity, i.e., 
    $\lim_{t \to \infty} \| v_i(t) - v_0(t) \| = 0$, $\forall ~i \in \mathcal{N}$.
\end{enumerate}
